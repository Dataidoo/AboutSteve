PDFs in.
Spreadsheet out.

A tiny script that eats a folder of PDFs and spits out a tidy, sortable, fully-yours Excel file.

Download PDF2Sheets .zip · ~14kb

How it works

In an oversimplified 6 steps.

1

Double-click the bat.

A folder picker pops up. select a folder with PDF's watch it process and report.

run_parser.bat
2

Meet your spreadsheet.

An Excel file appears with unlabelled rows showing in red so you know exactly where to look.

PDF2Sheets_Review.xlsx
3

Tag a few favourites.

Sort by any means, then use the dropdowns to assign Main and Sub categories to your row data.

Main · Sub
4

Run it again.

Your labels are remembered. The next pass auto-fills everything matching what you already taught it.

merchant_list.txt
5

Repeat.

Keep going till everything wears a label and your columns have been edited to suit your needs.

no more red
6

Export & enjoy.

When you're happy, run the export. You get a clean file ready for whatever dashboard, or "next step" you may have.

Dashboard_Export.xlsx
